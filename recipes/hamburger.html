Hamburger

A delectable treat, great for family outings!

The hamburger from the hit tv show Spongebob square pants is finally here. I was rewatching the show and I stumbled upon this burger and just had to bring it to life. With the most mouth watering meat with the oozing cheese and firm bread.

After eating the hamburger your life will change forever. Do not forget to share it with the family! Your kids will love it, especially if they watch Spongebob.

Ingredients

  • Finest bread
  • Sticks
  • Hair
  • Dead Skin Cells
  • Moss
  • Pimple Residue
  • Lettuce
  • Melted Cheese!
  • Toe Nail Clippings
  • Patty

Instructions

  1. Add the melted cheese to the bottom patty.
  2. Sprinkle the toe nail clippings in the sizzling cheese.
  3. Put the strands of hair on top of the cheese.
  4. Wrap the patty in moss, then place it on the cheese.
  5. Get sticks and jam it through the moss and place some on top of the moss.
  6. Place the lettuce on the moss.
  7. Sprinkle the dead skin cells all over the lettuce.
  8. On the top patty turn it on its head and spread the pimple residue all over it.
  9. Close the burger and enjoy!
