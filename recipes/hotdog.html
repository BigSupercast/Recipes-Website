Hotdog

The most scrumptious hotdog! Extermely satisfying!!!

Need a quick dinner for you and your family, dont be alarmed! you could make a quick meal with minimal effort, that still taste finominale!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • 2 Hotdogs
  • White Bread
  • Mustard

Instructions

  1. Boil water, and cook the hotdogs in it.
  2. Slice the hotdogs and lay them making an oval in the center of them.
  3. Add the rice in the middle of the hotdogs, use the hotdogs as a bowl.
  4. Squrit mustard all over them dogs.
